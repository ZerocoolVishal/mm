About

Anupama Tejuja

Psychotherapist and Hypnotherapist

My approach to psychotherapy focuses on deepening your working knowledge of human behavior in order to foster more independent and better-informed decisions in both your personal and professional life. I have worked using psychodynamic counseling that focuses on the unconscious mind and past experiences and explores their influences on current behavior. There is a constant interaction between conscious and unconscious thoughts, feelings, wishes, urges, and fantasies.

Success is measured not only in relief from current symptoms but the increasing capacity to know yourself more fully, to find greater satisfaction in what you do and feel more fulfilled in your relationships.

My framework is eclectic- I draw from a variety of psychodynamic therapies attuned to the specific needs of each client. I am a certified consulting hypnotherapist certified by the National Guild of Hypnotism, USA. I am also a board-certified Psychologist and Hypnotist by the International Board of Hypnotism, Education, and Certification, USA. Hypnosis can be used for a variety of problems from smoking cessation,weight-loss to past-life regression. Sometimes to get Catharsis- which is an emotional release from a problem, there is a need to go back to an earlier time, maybe even an earlier life.

I have learned Past-Life Regression from Dr. Brian Weiss in New York, USA. A lot of our memories are so deeply rooted in our subconscious and affect us without us being aware of them The goal of past life therapy is to go back, relive those memories and release them-thereby healing oneself. I treat each person holistically and do not treat persons simply in terms of their symptoms or diagnosis. The body has an innate intelligence to heal itself and no disease can be cured without the full cooperation of the mind.
